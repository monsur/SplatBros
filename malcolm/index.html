Here you can learn some of the basics to the trading card game Magic: the Gathering.
You might have heard that Magic is a very complicated game. In some ways it is, but once you learn the basics, it is much easier to dig deeper into the game.

First you need a deck. You can get some cheap ones on amazon if you look it up.
Once you have that, the goal is to reduce the opponent/opponets life total to 0. Each player starts with 20 life.
